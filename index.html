Process

Explore Our Process

We see every project as an opportunity to create environmentally responsible and inspiring spaces. Our approach balances functionality, sustainability, and client collaboration to achieve meaningful designs.

Pre Design

The initial phase focuses on understanding the site and client objectives. We conduct thorough site analysis, evaluating topography, climate, and ecological factors. Simultaneously, we refine the project scope, budget, and timeline while researching building codes and contextual influences.

Schematic Design

We explore multiple design concepts, using sketches and 3D models to shape the project vision. This phase integrates passive solar strategies, natural ventilation, and local materials, ensuring efficiency and harmony with the environment. Client feedback helps refine the best approach.

Design Development

The selected design evolves with detailed structural, mechanical, and spatial refinements. Close collaboration with consultants, engineers, and clients ensures functionality, sustainability, and adherence to project goals. Early contractor involvement helps assess feasibility and costs.

Construction Documentation & Administration

We produce precise construction documents that outline structural integrity, material selections, and compliance with building regulations. During construction, we provide oversight, address site queries, and ensure seamless execution to maintain design intent.

Handover

A final walkthrough ensures quality and attention to detail. We verify that the project meets design and performance expectations before officially handing it over, ensuring a successful transition from construction to occupancy.
