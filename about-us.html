About the founder- Sanchit Jhamb

Sanchit Jhamb is not just an architect—he is a storyteller who brings spaces to life. As the Principal Architect of 7F Design, he curates experiences, blending form, function, and emotion into every project. A graduate of Gateway College of Architecture and Design, he honed his skills under India's finest architects, gaining deep insights into how spaces shape human interactions.

For Sanchit, architecture is not just about structures but about crafting identities and translating dreams into reality. His designs are a harmony of precision and poetry, where every shadow, texture, and proportion serves a purpose. Whether a bespoke residence or a grand commercial space, his creations evoke emotions that linger beyond the walls.

Under his leadership, 7F Design is redefining architectural storytelling, leaving a timeless imprint of creativity, craftsmanship, and conviction on every skyline it touches.
